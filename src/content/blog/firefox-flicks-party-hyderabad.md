It's party time!

The Firefox Flicks '12 contest has come to an end and the [results](https://firefoxflicks.mozilla.org/) are out. All the entries were just amazing and our judges had a tough time picking winners.

Take a look at the [showreel](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XVdN3gH9d64) of the best submissions!

## The Party

Mozilla India has planned a Flicks Party at [News Cafe](http://www.newscafe.co.in/), Hyderabad on Saturday 26th May to celebrate the success of the contest. We'll be screening all the winning videos followed by music and dinner.

We're also expecting a special guest — **Anahita Gottipati**, who won a prize for the Bay Area Video Coalition category.

Come join us and be part of the celebration.

Entry is by invitation only due to limited space. If interested, contact Galaxy [@GalaxyK](http://twitter.com/GalaxyK) or Vineel [@vineelreddy](http://twitter.com/vineelreddy) for details.

Thanks to everyone who took the time to make and submit a video to help share the story of Firefox with the world. Every entry made the contest better.
