*"Mozilla helped make the Web more and more awesome in 2012. Social API & Facebook integration, Firefox Marketplace, Webmaker, Firefox OS and a lot more. We're excited to share some of the highlights of Mozilla this year in facts, figures and pictures. It's all part of our commitment to innovating on behalf of people everywhere and continuing to build a brighter future for the Web in 2013 and beyond."*

What a year it was. Firefox Marketplace launching meant the open web could now be an app platform. Webmaker made it easier than ever to teach and learn web skills. And Firefox OS was rapidly becoming a real thing — a smartphone OS built entirely on web technologies.

2012 felt like Mozilla firing on all cylinders. The community in India was growing fast, events were happening everywhere, and there was genuine excitement about what the open web could become.

Here's to 2013 being even bigger.
