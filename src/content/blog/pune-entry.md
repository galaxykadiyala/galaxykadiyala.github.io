It's been a while since I actually wrote about myself. So, thought I'd write about my college life beginning.

Like most people, I wanted to go abroad for higher education. But personal problems meant that wasn't happening. So I applied to the only college in India with the specialization I wanted, gave my entrance exam, and after a long wait — got admission.

## Arrival

Packed my things and Pune, here I came.

The bus dropped me at a completely deserted spot. There was only one auto driver standing there. I asked him how far IT Park was — he said about 2KM and asked me to pay **400 rupees**.

I had just traveled 600KM by bus for 700 rupees, and he wanted 400 for 2KM.

After a long negotiation the rate was fixed at 200 rupees and I finally made it to Infotech Park.

Pune: Day 1. Not off to a great start, but here we go.
