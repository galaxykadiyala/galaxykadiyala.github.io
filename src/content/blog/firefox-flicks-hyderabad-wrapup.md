Whoa! That was awesome.

90 attendees — Mozilla Reps, Student Reps, geeks, entrepreneurs, students, media, and friends — at the Flicks party. The party began at 1700 hrs with a bang, opening with the 'About Mozilla' video, followed by the Firefox Flicks intro and the showreel.

## Special Guest

We had our special guest Anahita Gottipati, who was sweet enough to share about her Flicks experience and interest in filmmaking. Check out the article published about Firefox Flicks and Anahita in *The Hindu*, the leading national newspaper.

## The Night

The audience enjoyed watching all the winning videos screened at the venue, and the night kicked off with food, drinks (vendor: News Cafe), music, and mingling.

The fun moment of the night? The audience playing **dumb charades** guessing movie names — winners got Mozilla swag. Who doesn't love Mozilla swag?

Mozilla Reps and Student Reps at the event included Sai Kiran, Srikar Ananthula, Santosh Kesiraju, Kartheek, and Aloke Anand among others.

We had an amazing time watching the winning videos and meeting everyone. Thanks to all for coming and making the event a success.
