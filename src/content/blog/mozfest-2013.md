MozFest is unlike any other conference. It's participatory, chaotic, and inspiring in equal measure. Three floors of sessions, workshops, and hallway conversations that blur together into one sustained high.

## The Format

No passive keynote watching. Every session is hands-on. You build, you hack, you teach. The people running sessions are peers, not authorities.

## What I Took Away

A renewed conviction that the open web is worth fighting for. And a notebook full of ideas I'm still working through.

## The Community, Again

It keeps coming back to the people. MozFest is proof that when you gather the right humans around a shared mission, something real happens.
