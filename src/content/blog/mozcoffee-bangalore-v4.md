MozCoffee v4.0 was held on Feb 16, 2014 at Matteo Coffee, Bangalore.

After three disastrous MozCoffee meetups, finally we had Mozillians joining us at our fourth MozCoffee!

Thanks to Bram Pitoyo for joining us. Bram works for Mozilla New Zealand as a UX designer and was in town for the Meta Refresh conference.

We had two Mozilla Reps and 5 Mozillians at the meet-up. We didn't have a proper agenda prepared but had a great chat with Bram about Marketplace, Firefox OS, SuMo, UX, FSA, and ways to contribute for fresh Mozillians.

## Firefox OS in the Wild

Bram, Kaustav and I walked like crazy on the streets of Bangalore to find a mobile shop doing Firefox OS research. It was a fun adventure.

Firefox Student Ambassadors is very popular among college students. We were getting a lot of requests from FSAs to conduct events at their colleges — a campus event in Bangalore was already in the works.

## Takeaway

Good coffee, tummy full of potatoes, loud music, great discussions, ways to contribute, awesome time. That's a MozCoffee.
