We had a [Firefox Flicks](http://firefoxflicks.org/) screening event in Bangalore on Sunday the 15th April. A lot of people are excited about Flicks — it really gets the creativity out of people.

I contacted folks who are working in the film and animation industry and we all gathered at a restaurant on a fine evening.

## The Evening

I showed them the Flicks trailer and a couple of prize-winning videos from previous years to kick things off. Then gave a brief rundown — different categories, countries, rules, and the prizes.

For a moment everyone was surprised by the prizes on offer. Drinks and food at this place were also awesome, so there was a good vibe.

Everyone started sharing their previous experiences with short films they'd worked on. One of the guys has already started shooting a ZooZoo-style video (remember those Vodafone ads?) titled **"Why is Firefox So Fast?"** — can't wait to see how that turns out.

Hoping for some great video submissions from India. Come on, show your talent to the world!
