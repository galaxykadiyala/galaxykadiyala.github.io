Big news — Firefox 4 is now available for Android and Maemo!

This is a huge moment for the open web on mobile. For the first time, Android users have access to a truly capable, standards-compliant browser that isn't tied to a single platform's goals or business model.

Firefox 4 on Android brings the full desktop browsing experience to mobile — tabbed browsing, add-ons support, Firefox Sync to keep your bookmarks and passwords in sync across devices, and all the HTML5 and CSS3 goodness that makes the modern web work.

For Mozilla, mobile was always a critical frontier. The web needed a champion on phones just as much as on desktops. This release makes that real.

Download it from the Android Market and give it a go. The open web belongs in your pocket.
