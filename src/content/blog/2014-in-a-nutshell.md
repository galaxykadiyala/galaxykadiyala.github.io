2014 was a packed year. Between Firefox OS app days, Mozilla community meetups, and a whole lot of travel, here's a quick recap of what went down.

## Mozilla Highlights

The Firefox OS ecosystem really started maturing in 2014. Watching a phone run a web-native OS felt like the future arriving. I spent a lot of time helping people understand what Firefox OS meant for the open web.

## Community Events

The Mozilla India community meetup was one of the highlights of the year — getting the whole community in one room, exchanging ideas, and planning for the year ahead. These moments remind me why community work matters.

## Looking Ahead

2015 is going to be interesting. The web keeps evolving, the community keeps growing. Let's see what we can build together.
