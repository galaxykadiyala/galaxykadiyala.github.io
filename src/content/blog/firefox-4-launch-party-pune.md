Firefox 4 is here and Pune celebrated in style.

The launch party brought together Mozilla Reps, Student Ambassadors, developers, and open web enthusiasts for an evening of demos, discussions, and genuine excitement about what Firefox 4 brings to the table.

## Firefox 4 Highlights

Firefox 4 was a big release — a complete redesign, massive performance improvements, and serious HTML5 and CSS3 support. It finally felt like the browser had caught up with and surpassed the competition.

Features that had everyone excited:
- The new streamlined UI
- Hardware-accelerated graphics
- Firefox Sync built in
- WebM video support
- The Do Not Track privacy feature

## The Community Energy

Launch parties are about more than the software. They're about the community that builds and promotes it. Getting people in a room, showing them what Firefox can do, and sending them away as advocates — that's the real product of an event like this.

Pune's Mozilla community was growing fast at this point. Events like this kept the momentum going and brought in new contributors who went on to do great things.

Here's to many more.
