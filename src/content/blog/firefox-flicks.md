Firefox Flicks is a video making contest. Here's the brief:

*Firefox Flicks gives budding filmmakers the opportunity to create and submit short videos promoting Firefox. The contest runs worldwide with winners chosen from four regions — the U.S. and Canada, Latin America, Europe, and Asia/Africa/Australia/New Zealand.*

Check the full details at [firefoxflicks.mozilla.org](https://firefoxflicks.mozilla.org/en-US/creative).

## Why You Should Enter

If you have an idea that could make a great Firefox video — shoot it and submit before **May 1st**.

The prizes are genuinely incredible. One of them is **$60,000 (USD)** towards rental of Panavision camera, lenses and accessories for four weeks — plus an **exclusive trip to the Cannes film festival**. That was just added in the last week. Seriously, enter.
